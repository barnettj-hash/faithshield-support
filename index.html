FAQ

The app starts but audio sounds distorted.

Confirm device volume is not maxed and Music/SFX are enabled in the Game Hub settings. Restart the app after changing audio settings.

My progress or name did not appear.

FAITHSHIELD stores progress locally on your device. If the app is removed and reinstalled, local progress may reset.

A story preview appears blank.

Close and reopen the preview, then restart the app once. If the issue continues, send the story name and device model in your support request.
