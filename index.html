FAITHSHIELD Support

FAITHSHIELD is an interactive Bible learning game focused on trivia, reflection journaling, memorization, and story-based adventure from Genesis to David.

This page provides gameplay help, troubleshooting, and support contact details.

Core Experience

  • Scripture-based challenge stages
  • Difficulty options: Easy, Medium, Advanced
  • Memory Verse Trainer and Reflection Journal
  • Daily thought and practical action prompt

Technical Support

  • iPhone and iPad support
  • Audio/music controls in Game Hub
  • Saved profile name and progress tracking
  • Story preview narration and stage unlocks

FAQ

The app starts but audio sounds distorted.

Confirm device volume is not maxed and Music/SFX are enabled in the Game Hub settings. Restart the app after changing audio settings.

My progress or name did not appear.

FAITHSHIELD stores progress locally on your device. If the app is removed and reinstalled, local progress may reset.

A story preview appears blank.

Close and reopen the preview, then restart the app once. If the issue continues, send the story name and device model in your support request.

Contact Support

Email: support@faithshield.app

Include your device model, iOS version, and a screenshot/video when reporting issues.
