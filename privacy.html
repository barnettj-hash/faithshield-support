FAITHSHIELD Privacy Policy

This page explains what information FAITHSHIELD stores, how gameplay data is handled, and what choices players have when using the app and desktop experience.

Effective date: February 2026

Privacy at a Glance

Stored Locally

Player name, progress, settings, streaks, and activity state are kept on the device.

No Selling of Data

FAITHSHIELD is not designed to sell personal information or share gameplay data for advertising.

Your Control

You can reset progress in the app or remove the app to clear local gameplay data.

Information We Collect

FAITHSHIELD is designed for Bible learning gameplay. Information handled by the app may include:

  • Player name entered in-app
  • Local gameplay progress such as levels, XP, settings, streaks, and activity state

How Data Is Stored

FAITHSHIELD stores gameplay data locally on your device. This data is used to preserve your in-app experience between sessions and support progress through the game.

Data Sharing

FAITHSHIELD does not sell personal data. The app is not intended to share user gameplay data with third parties for advertising.

Children's Privacy

Parents or guardians should supervise children using mobile apps and review device privacy settings.

Your Choices

You can reset in-app progress using in-game reset controls or by removing the app from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@faithshield.app.
