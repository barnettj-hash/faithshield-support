FAITHSHIELD Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026

FAITHSHIELD is designed for Bible learning gameplay. This policy explains what data is handled by the app.

Information We Collect

  • Player name entered in-app
  • Local gameplay progress (levels, XP, settings, streaks, and activity state)

How Data Is Stored

FAITHSHIELD stores gameplay data locally on your device. This data is used to preserve your in-app experience between sessions.

Data Sharing

FAITHSHIELD does not sell personal data. The app is not intended to share user gameplay data with third parties for advertising.

Children's Privacy

Parents or guardians should supervise children using mobile apps and review device privacy settings.

Your Choices

You can reset in-app progress using in-game reset controls or by removing the app from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@faithshield.app
